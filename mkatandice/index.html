Island One

This represents the standard dice version of the game in which players have 15 turns to accumulate the most amount of points possible by rolling the special dice.

This is best played by 1-4 players (for multi-player games, just share this app with your friends!).

Island Two

This represents the "plus" version of the game which more closely mirrors the classic boardgame. Players accumulate victory points in a battle of "first to 10".

This is best played by 2-4 players (for multi-player games, just share this app with your friends!).
